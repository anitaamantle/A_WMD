👩🏽‍🔧 Meet the Founder – Anita

Hey, I’m Anita — the proud founder of Gearhead Collective! This platform was born out of pure love for cars and a dream to connect with others who share that same fire. 💙

My passion for cars started when I was little, watching my dad take care of his beloved Toyota Corolla. I remember being amazed at how a simple car could have so much character, especially after I saw videos online of modified Corollas looking absolutely stunning and street-ready.

That sparked something deep inside me. I started learning about car culture, watching builds, reading articles, and dreaming of starting my own collective. I realized there were so many people like me who love stance, speed, power, and expression through their rides.

So I built Gearhead Collective — a home for us all. Whether you’re into GTIs, Lambos, 6x6 monsters, or just love the vibe — this space is yours too. Welcome to the family.

🔥 Our Mission

Gearhead Collective exists to bring car lovers together – whether you're into classics, track beasts, or off-road monsters. We're a family built on passion, power, and connection.

🚗 Our Vision

To become the number one online community for Botswana's car enthusiasts – uniting diverse subcultures through events, knowledge sharing, and automotive creativity.

📍 Where It All Started

What began as a local meet-up of GTI fans in Gaborone quickly grew into a vibrant movement – a collective of street racers, stance fanatics, and 4x4 adventurers who live and breathe motorsport culture.

🛠️ The Team

Our squad is made up of young car lovers, builders, designers, and event organizers. We're students, tuners, dreamers—and this community is powered by YOU.
