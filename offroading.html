Trail Highlights in South Africa & Botswana

De Wildt 4x4 Trail – Gauteng, South Africa

Located near Hartbeespoort, this scenic 4x4 route offers rocky paths, steep climbs, and stunning views of the Magaliesberg. Perfect for beginners and experienced off-roaders alike.

More info on AllTrails

Kgale Hill Trails – Gaborone, Botswana

Right outside Gaborone, Kgale Hill’s off-road routes combine dust, dirt, and steep slopes with breathtaking vistas. Great for weekend explorers or meet-ups.
